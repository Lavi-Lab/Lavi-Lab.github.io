Assignments (45%)

  • Individual-based Assignments (15% for each)

    • There will be three assignments (Question Sets or Coding) in total. All assignments must be done independently by yourself.

  • Format

    • You are encouraged to either typeset your solution in LaTeX or Word, or scan or take photos of your handwritten solution (please bundle your scans/photos into a single PDF file)

  • Submission

    • If there is no coding involved, please submit your answers as a single PDF file. Otherwise, please compress the PDF file with related codes into a ZIP file. The PDF file should be accepted by VeriGuide before submission. All the submissions should be made via Blackboard by 11:59pm

Course Policy

  • Academic Honesty Policy

    • The Chinese University of Hong Kong places very high importance on honesty in academic work submitted by students, and adopts a policy of zero tolerance on academic dishonesty.

    All submissions (e.g., assignments) should be processed by VeriGuide first.

  • Submission Policy

    • Assignments, reports, and presentation slides cannot be plagiarized from the Internet.

    The use of AI tools (e.g., Chat-GPT) is allowed for assistance, but must be explicitly acknowledged.

  • Late Submission Policy

    • For all the submissions (e.g., assignments), late submission are subject to score deduction:

    1. Late by less than 12 hours -- deducted by 20%
    2. Late by 12 to 24 hours -- deducted by 40%
    3. Late by 24 to 36 hours -- deducted by 60%
    4. Late by 36 to 48 hours -- deducted by 80%
    5. Submissions that are late by more than 48 hours will not be accepted.
